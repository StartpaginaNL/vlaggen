Flag Quiz – Learn, Practice & Play World Flags

The free online flag quiz for all ages. Learn and practice the flags of all 190 countries in the world. Play solo against the clock or challenge a friend in 2-player duel mode. Available in 11 languages.

What is Flag Quiz?

Flag Quiz is a free, browser-based geography game that helps you learn to recognize the national flags of every country in the world. Whether you're a student studying for a geography exam, a traveller who wants to know the flags of the countries you visit, or simply a trivia lover — Flag Quiz is the perfect tool to practice and test your knowledge.

The game is completely free to play. No account or sign-up is required. Simply open the site and start playing immediately.

How does Flag Quiz work?

Each round you are shown a national flag and given four answer options. You must identify the correct country before the 20-second timer runs out. The key feature: the faster you answer, the more points you earn. An instant correct answer scores 100 points, while a correct answer with 1 second left scores just 10 points. This makes every second count!

Solo Quiz Mode

Play through 20 random flag questions. After each correct answer the game automatically advances to the next question so you keep your momentum. At the end of the quiz you see your total score, which you can save to the global leaderboard.

2-Player Duel Mode

Challenge a friend on the same device. Players take turns identifying flags — each with their own 20-second timer. Every player starts with 2 lives. A wrong answer or a timeout costs a life. The first player to lose both lives loses the duel. The winner's score is saved to the leaderboard.

Browse Mode

Not ready to quiz yet? Browse all 190 flags organized by continent. Click any flag to see the country name, capital city, continent, and flag colors. Use the search bar or continent filter to find specific flags.

Scoring System

Flag Quiz uses a speed-based scoring system designed to reward quick recognition:

  • 100 points — answered within the first second
  • 55 points — answered in 10 seconds
  • 10 points — answered with 1 second remaining
  • 0 points — timed out or answered incorrectly

The maximum possible score in a 20-question solo quiz is 2,000 points. Can you get a perfect score?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flag Quiz free?

Yes. Flag Quiz is completely free to play. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no sign-up required.

How many flags are in the quiz?

The quiz includes all 190 internationally recognized sovereign states. Every country has its own flag, which is displayed as a crisp SVG image.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. Flag Quiz is fully responsive and works on all modern smartphones, tablets, and desktop browsers.

How do I save my score?

After completing a solo quiz or winning a duel, you will be prompted to enter your name and save your score. Scores are stored locally in your browser and shown on the homepage leaderboard.

What is the 2-player duel mode?

Two players share one device and take turns answering flag questions. Each player has 2 lives. Make 2 mistakes and you lose. It's a fast-paced way to compete with friends or family.
